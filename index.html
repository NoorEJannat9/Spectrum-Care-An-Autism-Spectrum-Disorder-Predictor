Behavioral Assessment

1. Does your child face difficulties in focusing or maintaining attention on tasks for extended periods?
2. Does your child tend to fixate on specific details or interests, making it hard to focus on broader concepts?
3. Does your child get easily overwhelmed by distractions, making it difficult to concentrate or focus?
4. Does your child struggle with multi-tasking or managing multiple instructions at once?
5. Does your child often get distracted during tasks, making it challenging for them to concentrate on activities?
6. Does your child have trouble making eye contact during conversations or interactions with others?
7. Does your child prefer to play alone rather than engaging with other children?
8. Does your child engage in repetitive behaviors, such as hand-flapping or rocking?
9. Does your child have difficulty understanding or using non-verbal communication, such as facial expressions or gestures?
10. Does your child seem to have a limited range of interests or play activities, preferring certain objects or routines?
11. Does your child become upset by small changes in their routine or environment?
12. Does your child struggle with pretend play or imaginative activities?
13. Does your child have strong preferences for specific textures, smells, or sounds, reacting unusually to them?
14. Does your child struggle to understand or express their emotions appropriately?
15. Does your child frequently engage in behaviors or activities that are hard to interrupt or redirect?
16. Does your child exhibit a high level of sensitivity to certain sensory experiences, such as loud noises or bright lights?
17. Does your child often focus on small details of objects or situations, ignoring the overall context?
18. Does your child show limited awareness of danger or lack of response to important environmental cues?
19. Does your child have difficulty building and maintaining relationships with peers or adults?
20. Does your child show intense focus or repetitive actions with specific objects, such as lining up toys or turning objects in circles?
